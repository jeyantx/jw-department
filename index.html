Urapakkam Congregation - Department Assignment

Select a date and click Add Schedule to begin

How it works

Adding Meetings

Pick a date using the date picker, then click Add Schedule to add a meeting column. You can add up to 8 meetings per schedule. Click remove under a date to remove it.

Filling Assignments

Each brother can fill data for their department — Sound, Attendant, or Cleaning. Select the assigned brother or cleaning group from the dropdown for each role and meeting.

Save

Once saved, all data is stored in the backend. When you reload the page, all data is fetched and displayed automatically.

Clear + Save

When you clear and then save, all data is erased from the backend. This resets the schedule completely.

Sharing & Printing

Once everything is filled, you can:

  • Print — generates a clean A4 landscape PDF for printout
  • Copy Image — copies the schedule to clipboard, ready to paste in WhatsApp or any app
  • Download — saves the schedule as a PNG image to share in WhatsApp group

Conflicts

Toggle Conflicts to highlight when the same brother is assigned to multiple roles on the same meeting date.
